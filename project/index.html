Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about credit stacking.

What is credit stacking?

Credit stacking is your launch strategy - combining multiple business credit accounts to maximize funding while building your business properly from day one. We help with formation, EIN, business banking, and credit - everything needed to start strong.

How long does it take to build a credit stack?

Most businesses can establish $50,000-$100,000 in credit lines within 30-60 days. Larger credit stacks ($250,000+) typically take 90-120 days as we strategically build your credit profile to qualify for higher limits.

Do I need personal credit for business credit stacking?

No, a key benefit of business credit stacking is establishing credit that's separate from your personal credit. We focus on building your business credit profile without personal guarantees whenever possible.

What's the difference between credit stacking and a business loan?

Unlike a lump-sum loan, credit stacking gives you multiple revolving credit lines that you can use as needed. This provides continuous access to capital rather than a one-time infusion, typically with better terms and no collateral requirements.

Can startups qualify for credit stacking?

Absolutely! Our Startup Launch Program handles everything from business formation to first funding. We'll establish your EIN, business bank account, vendor credit lines, and initial credit cards - giving you the foundation to grow properly from day one.
