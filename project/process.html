Detailed Process Overview

A deeper look at how we help businesses establish and grow their credit.

1. Initial Consultation & Application

After your initial application, one of our credit specialists will review your business information and schedule a consultation to understand your funding needs and goals.

  • 5-minute online application
  • Soft credit pull (no impact to score)
  • 30-minute strategy session

2. Business Credit Profile Analysis

We conduct a comprehensive analysis of your current business credit profile and identify the best opportunities for credit stacking.

  • Dun & Bradstreet file review
  • Experian & Equifax business credit reports
  • Vendor credit history assessment

3. Credit Stack Strategy Development

Based on your business credit profile, we develop a customized credit stacking strategy to maximize your funding potential.

  • Customized lender matching
  • Optimal application sequencing
  • Credit limit maximization plan

4. Implementation & Funding

Our team manages the entire application process and helps you activate your new credit lines when approved.

  • Application submission
  • Follow-up with lenders
  • Credit line activation
